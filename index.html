Our Core Consulting Functions

Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE)

We design and implement tailored HSE systems with a focus on regulatory compliance, risk mitigation, and workforce safety.

Specialised skills include:

  • HSE audits, inspections, and reporting
  • Occupational Health and Safety policy development
  • Incident investigation and post-accident analysis
  • Training, inductions, and statutory compliance programmes
  • Sector-specific safety solutions for telecommunications and engineering environments

Human Resources (HR)

We help organisations build strong, compliant, and high-performing teams through efficient HR systems and people-first strategies.

Specialised skills include:

  • Talent acquisition, onboarding, and workforce planning
  • Internal employee conflict resolution and CCMA representation
  • Employment contracts, leave management, and UIF administration
  • Compliance with EEA, BCEA, and bargaining council requirements
  • Skills development planning and transformation advisory

Business Management

We align operational strategy with business goals to improve efficiency, reduce risk, and enable sustainable growth.

Specialised skills include:

  • Strategic planning and operational leadership
  • Procurement, inventory control, and cash budgeting
  • Compliance and risk management systems
  • Project coordination and stakeholder reporting
  • Business continuity and change management integration

At Undefined Solutions, we integrate technical expertise and practical experience across three core functions to deliver holistic, continuity-focused consulting services.

About Undefined Solutions

Founded in 2019, Undefined Solutions began as a specialised Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE) consultancy serving South Africa’s telecommunications sector. Built on deep industry knowledge and hands-on experience, we’ve since evolved into a multi-disciplinary consulting partner, offering integrated solutions across HSE, Human Resources, and Business Management. Our mission is to help organisations achieve operational excellence and long-term sustainability through compliance, strategic alignment, and continuity-focused support.
